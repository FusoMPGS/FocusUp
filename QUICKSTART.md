# 🎯 FocusUp - Quick Start Guide

## What is FocusUp?
FocusUp is your personal study companion - a free web app that combines focus music, a smart timer, and breathing exercises to help you study better and manage stress.

## 🚀 Quick Start (2 minutes)

1. **Open the App**
   - Open `index.html` in your web browser
   - You'll see the home page with 3 main features

2. **Pick Your Music**
   - Click "Music" in the menu
   - Choose from 5 focus playlists (Lo-Fi, Ambient, Nature, Classical, Jazz)
   - Click Play to start

3. **Start Timer**
   - Click "Focus Timer" in the menu
   - Click "Start" for a 25-minute focused work session
   - App will notify you when time's up

4. **Stay Calm**
   - Feeling stressed? Click "Breathing" in the menu
   - Click "Start Exercise" and follow the circle
   - Complete 4 breathing cycles (about 1.5 minutes)

## 🎯 The Pomodoro Method (Used in FocusUp Timer)
- **25 minutes**: Deep focus work
- **5 minutes**: Short break (walk, stretch, rest eyes)
- **Repeat**: 4 cycles, then take longer 15-30 minute break

## 🌬️ Breathing Technique Used

**4-7-8 Breathing** (Scientifically proven to reduce anxiety)
- Breathe IN for 4 seconds
- Hold breath for 7 seconds  
- Breathe OUT for 8 seconds
- Repeat 4 times = ~1.5 minutes

Perfect for:
- Anxiety before exams
- Stress during difficult work
- Mental clarity needed
- Quick reset between tasks

## 💡 Pro Tips for Best Results

✅ **DO:**
- Use the same playlist every study session (builds habit)
- Take real breaks (not just scrolling)
- Stay hydrated while studying
- Use the breathing exercise when overwhelmed
- Study in chunks (not marathon sessions)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Skip breaks - they're essential!
- Have phone notifications on - silence them
- Study more than 2 hours without a longer break
- Ignore the breathing exercise if stressed
- Use your break time for more work

## 🆘 Common Questions

**Q: The music doesn't sound real?**
A: FocusUp uses synthesized tones for demo purposes. Real audio features can be added later. Focus is on the timer and breathing! 

**Q: My progress disappeared?**
A: FocusUp stores data in your browser. If you cleared browser data, it will be reset. Your stats are:
- Private to your computer
- Saved locally (not on any server)
- Lost only if you clear browser data

**Q: Can I change the 25-minute timer?**
A: Yes! In the Focus Timer section, change "Focus Time (min)" to any value you want.

**Q: Which playlist is best?**
A: Try them all! Different playlists work for different people and study types. Experiment!

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes! FocusUp works on all devices with a browser. Open this file on your phone!

## 🔄 Your First Study Session

1. Open FocusUp
2. Go to **Music** → Select Lo-Fi Beats → Click **Play**
3. Go to **Focus Timer** → Click **Start**
4. Study for 25 minutes without checking phone
5. Timer notifies you automatically
6. Take a 5-minute break
7. App auto-starts next cycle
8. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-minute break
9. Repeat!
