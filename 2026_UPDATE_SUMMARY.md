### 6. **ADVANCED STUDY TECHNIQUES** 🧠
**New: Science-Backed Methods**

**Active Recall**
- Test yourself instead of re-reading
- 50% better retention than passive reading
- Use quizzes to strengthen memory

**Spaced Repetition**
- Review at optimal intervals: 1 day → 3 days → 1 week → 2 weeks
- Fights the forgetting curve
- Perfect for long-term retention

**Mind Mapping**
- Visualize connections between concepts
- Your brain remembers images better than text
- Great for complex topics

**Interleaving**
- Mix different topics during study
- Improves problem-solving skills
- Better than blocking similar problems
